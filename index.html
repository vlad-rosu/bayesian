About

Bayesian Case Competition is a case competition on financial risk management where 12-15 teams of contestants compete to build a developed solution for an open-ended case on market and investment risk mitigation to be presented to a panel of judges. This year's prize pool will consist of $1,800 in cash prize and other gift prizes. Bayesian 2018 will take place on January 27th at the University of Waterloo.
