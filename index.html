About

Bayesian Case Competition is a case competition on financial risk management where 12-15 teams of contestants compete to build a developed solution for an open-ended case on market and investment risk mitigation to be presented to a panel of judges.

This will be the second Bayesian Case Competition after an unforgettable successful event in 2018. In addition to the case competition the event day will also feature keynote speakers, workshops hosted by our corporate sponsors, an alumni panel consisting of alumni currently working in financial risk management. This year's prize pool will consist of $3,000+ in cash prizes and other gift prizes. Bayesian 2019 will take place on October 5th at the University of Waterloo.
